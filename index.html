About

I design the parts of a product where the decisions actually matter.

I start from the problem and the people doing the work, not the interface — understanding the system before the screen, where the real friction sits, and which trade-offs are worth making. The design follows from that.

I work closely with product and engineering, because the best decisions come from all three disciplines in the same room. Across cybersecurity, workforce operations, and AI-assisted products, the throughline holds: take something complex and make it feel like it isn't.
