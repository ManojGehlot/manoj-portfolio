About

I design the parts of a product that carry consequences.

Money, approvals, security, operations — the areas where a wrong screen becomes a real cost. Those are the problems I'm drawn to, and where careful design earns its keep.

My decisions start from the business problem and the people doing the work under deadline, not from a layout. Over 9+ years in enterprise SaaS I've learned that the best enterprise design is usually the least visible: it removes friction and error without asking anyone to notice.
